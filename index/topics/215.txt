Websites about uncommon and unusual pets.<p> \
\
The term "exotic pet" has several meanings:   <ul compact><li> an animal that cannot be owned without a permit    <li> an endangered species that cannot be legally owned    <li> a non-domesticated animal that cannot be truly tamed    <li> an uncommon pet that does not fall under any of the criteria above   </ul> \
\
When considering an exotic pet, it is very important to find out about the legality of owning the animal, its specific care-taking needs, the availability of veterinary care in your area, and the changing behavior and needs of the animal as it matures.<p>
